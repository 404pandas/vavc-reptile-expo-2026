The Team

Meet the Family

👩‍⚕️

Dr. Lisa Coder, DVM

Lead Veterinarian

Dr. Lisa graduated from the University of Missouri in 2013, bringing experience across equine, mixed animal, emergency, and USDA public health veterinary medicine. In 2021 she founded Home Vet — a holistic practice offering acupuncture, laser therapy, and herbal medicine. In 2023 that expanded into Vital Animal Veterinary Clinic. She has two children, four cats, and a gecko she describes as "the love of her life." Her approach: care, heal, thrive.

🩺

Abby

Registered Veterinary Technician

Graduated from Globe University in 2013 in Veterinary Technology. Began at All Animal Pet Hospital in 2015 where she discovered a deep passion for exotic animal medicine. Has 3 cats and a dog. Loves fishing and crafts.
